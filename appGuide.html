VACANCIES MENU

ADVICE AND WARNINGS

ADVICES

Advice 1: Applicants are advised to Read and Understand the Requirements before applying for a given position.
Advice 2: Applicants are advised to View Jobs Availability and Requirements before applying for a given position.
Advice 3: Applicants are advised to Sign-up in order to create and access your login credentials and details because applying for any Job Position requires applicants to login to the system first before be able to apply for his/her desired available position.
Advice 4: STRICTLY: No Applicants should apply for a job position twice. Failure to adhere to this instruction shall disqualify such applicants.
Advice 5: STRICTLY: No Applicants should apply for more than one job position. Failure to adhere to this instruction shall disqualify such applicants.


APPLICATION STEPS

"HOW TO APPLY"

COMPLETE STEPS

Step 1: Click on Sign-up on the Vacancies Menu.
Step 2: Fill the Sign-up Form accurately and completely and submit.
Note: All fields are compulsory to fill.
Step 3: Login with your FIRST NAME as Username and PHONE NO as your Password.
Step 4: Fill the Application Form accordingly, accurately and completely. All fields are compulsory to be filled by all applicants.
Step 5: Read through your choices on the form and be sure before submitting your Application Form.
Note: Filling the form is part of your screen exercise. There shall be no opportunity to Edit, Modify or Change anything on your form after submission.
