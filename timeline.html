SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT RELATED JOBS

REQUIREMENTS

B.Sc, B.Tech and HND

All Applicants must possess a minimum of B.Sc, B.Tech, and HND in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Programming and Informatics. Applicants that shall be considered must have a minimum of 5years working experience with a recognized IT/ICT based Organization or Government Parastatal. Applicants under this categories must also be able to prove their competency by showing at least two products or functional software they have developed single handedly as collaborative effort is not considered. These Applicants should bear in mind that Infosys Digital Solution shall investigates their claims so as to verify their authencity and qualifications.


SECRETARIAL RELATED JOBS

REQUIREMENTS

B.Sc, HND, ND, NCE

All Applicants applying for Secretarial Related Jobs such as Secretary, Receptionist, Driver, Messenger, Administrator, etc, shall be required to possess a minimum qualification of B.Sc, HND, ND and NCE in Secretariat Studies, Public Administration, Office Technology Management and others. This Job category requires a preresiquite 2years experience and any Customer Service experience or cognate experience shall be an added advantage. All Applicants with this category is expected to be Computer Literate with good knowledge in basic computer applications and operation with a good typing speed.


Special Considerations

Recommendations Privilege

Directors and Management staff members of Infosys Digital Solution have been given privilege to make recommendation on minor Job positions under this categories. The minor job position include: Cleaner, Messengers and Drivers. Infosys Digital Solution have made special consideration to the Applicants within this category. Contact the System Administrator in respect to this. Please use the Contact Us.

MASTERS, B.SC, B.TECH, HND

These Applicants should bear in mind that Infosys Digital Solution shall investigates their claims so as to verify their authencity and qualifications.


DATA MINERS AND SCIENTIST RELATED JOB

REQUIREMENTS

Masters, B.Sc, B.Tech, HND

PROBATION AND CONFIRMATION

INFOSYS POLICIES

ALL JOB CATEGORIES

All Applicants applying for one position or the other in the company shall upon successful completion of the recuritment process and recieving letter that indicates their Employment Status with Infosys Digital Solution; be place on a mandatory 1 Year Probation during which period Employed Applicant is expected to obtained Character Satistification and Job Competency Clearance from the company. These documents shall help the Probated Employed Applicant to obtain his/her Comfirmation of Appointment and Employment Letter at the end of the probation period.


RECOMMENDED APPLICANTS AND TERMINATION OF PROBATION

TO ALL APPLICANTS

All Applicants especially the recommended Applicants should aware that after a period of 6months into the probated period, if such applicants failed to prove himslef/herself worthy to complete the probated period based on investigated gross misconducts, unethnical behaviours and the likes, Infosys Digital Solution reserves the Legal Right to terminate such agreement binding the probated applicants with the company without any legal or non-legal compensation, obligation or consequencies.
